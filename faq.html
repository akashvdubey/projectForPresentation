Frequently Asked Questions

Que:What services do you offer?

Answer:For students we offer: laundry pick-up, wash and fold, and delivery; grocery shopping and pharmacy supply pick up; and dorm, suite and apartment cleaning. For non-students: we offer all of the same services. Prices for cleaning will be adjusted to suit the size of your abode.

Que:Is your detergent safe for sensitive skin? What about the environment?

Answer:We use mild detergent, recommended for sensitive skin. It is also a detergent that will not harm the environment.

Que:What other products will you use in my laundry?

Answer:We use a liquid fabric softener (also safe for sensitive skin/environment), a non-chlorine bleach/color booster (similar to Oxy Clean), and a dryer sheet fabric softener.

Que:Do you separate laundry by color?

Answer:Yes. We will separate lights from darks just as we would our own laundry. We will wash all items on a warm/cold setting to provide a clean that is gentle yet effective.

Que:Do you offer line/flat dry for gentle materials?

Answer:Yes, if you separate the laundry from the rest, we will follow your special instructions for a small additional fee.

Que:Do you iron?

Answer:Yes, if a piece is separated and marked for ironing, we will iron for a small additional fee.
